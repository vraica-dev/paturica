Art is, in essence, the deepest expression of human creativity. As difficult as it is to define, it is equally difficult to evaluate, considering that each artist chooses their own rules and working parameters. It can be said, however, that art is the result of choosing a medium, a set of rules for using that medium, and a set of values that determine what is worth expressing through that medium to induce a feeling, an idea, a sensation, or an experience as efficiently as possible for that medium. In its manifestation, art can also be considered a form of knowledge (artistic knowledge).
